Who We Are

HT Logistics LLC, founded in 2022 in Kansas City, is a fast-growing logistics company backed by over 20 years of leadership experience in retail and international logistics. We specialize in box truck transportation, freight coordination, and advanced dispatching, with an unwavering focus on safety, technology, and customer satisfaction. Our dedicated team provides personalized solutions to ensure every shipment is handled with care and delivered on time, supported by modern box trucks, experienced dispatchers, real-time tracking, and 24/7 assistance. Committed to transparency, reliability, and innovation, we simplify logistics so businesses can focus on growth while we manage transportation complexities. From local deliveries to cross-country freight and global expansion, HT Logistics is your trusted partner for efficient, tailored logistics solutions. Contact us today to experience logistics done right.
