Who We Are

HT Logistics LLC is a fast-growing logistics company founded in 2022 in Kansas City. Our leadership brings 20+ years of retail and international logistics expertise to help small and medium-sized businesses thrive.

We specialize in box truck transportation, freight coordination, and advanced dispatching, with a focus on safety, technology, and client satisfaction.

Our team is dedicated to providing personalized service, ensuring your cargo is handled with care and delivered on time, every time.

Whether you need local deliveries or cross-country freight solutions, HT Logistics is your trusted partner for all logistics needs.

Based in Kansas City, we serve clients across the Midwest and beyond, with a commitment to excellence and innovation in every shipment.
