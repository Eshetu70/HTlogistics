Who We Are

HT Logistics LLC, founded in 2022 in Kansas City, is a fast-growing logistics company backed by over 20 years of leadership experience in retail and international logistics. We specialize in box truck transportation, freight coordination, and advanced dispatching, with an unwavering focus on safety, technology, and customer satisfaction.

Committed to transparency, reliability, and innovation, we simplify logistics so businesses can focus on growth while we manage transportation complexities. From local deliveries to cross-country freight and global expansion, HT Logistics is your trusted partner for efficient, tailored logistics solutions.

HOTSHOT LOGISTICS

HT Logistics LLC is your go-to provider for hotshot logistics services, specializing in expedited transportation solutions for time-sensitive shipments. Our hotshot services are designed to meet the urgent needs of businesses requiring fast and reliable delivery.

With a dedicated team of logistics professionals and a fleet of modern vehicles, we ensure that your time-sensitive cargo is prioritized and delivered on schedule. Our hotshot logistics service is available 24/7, providing real-time tracking and updates to keep you informed every step of the way. Whether it's a last-minute delivery or a high-priority load, HT Logistics has you covered with our hotshot logistics solutions. We understand the importance of speed and reliability in hotshot logistics. Our team is available 24/7 to assist you with your urgent shipping needs.
